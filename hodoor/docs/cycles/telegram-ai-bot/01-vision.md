# Vision — telegram-ai-bot

## Scope mode
HOLD SCOPE

## 11-star version
A personal AI assistant that lives in your pocket via Telegram, accepting any input format (text, voice, photo, video) and orchestrating actions across your entire business stack. Starting with conversations, growing into a fully autonomous operations layer that sees, hears, and acts on your behalf.

## Commitment
Send any message (text, voice, photo, video) to a Telegram bot and get an AI-powered conversational response. Telegram handles multimodal capture natively.

## Not building
Intelligent media processing (image analysis, audio transcription, video understanding), business tool integrations (Odoo), autonomous actions, persistent memory, workflow automation, multi-user support, web UI

## Why this scope
The request is explicitly a bootstrap: get the simplest possible end-to-end pipe from mobile capture to AI response, validate the interaction pattern, then layer intelligence and integrations in future cycles.
